About

My name is Dominik and I live in Częstochowa, Poland. I'm in my last year of IT studies at Politechnika Częstochowska. I'm focusing mostly on the front-end.

I've spent majority of my life working in my family business, where I had a lot of different dutie's and also pretty much contact with client's. The business was related to all kind of cleaning service's like ensuring cleanliness in the shop centres, offices and also car dealership's. But I decided to change my carrier path so I signed up for college and started studying IT. Since the beginning, I knew that I want to be a Web Developer. I started from the totally basics, learning HTML, CSS and a little bit of Javascript. Then it was time for my first commercial project, which was business website for my old family company.

I could use bootstrap to simplify most of the work but I treatet this task as a good practise lesson so I wrote the whole code by myself, using HTML, Sass and also some Javascript. In my opinion this was a good lesson, over time I think that, the website could have more eye-catching design but as I said before, that was my first official project. From the pluses I can say, that the website is pretty fast and received very good SEO ranking, which is very important for businnes site.

I have done some other project's, which you can check below but none of them was commercial. I developed them just to practise my skills.

The "Front-End" industry moved forward very dynamically, nowadays big trio HTML&CSS&JS isn't enough even for a beginner so I decided to start learning VUE.js. It's definitely too early to say, that I have enough knowledge even for the basic's but I'm working on it:) At this moment I can say, that I'm familiar with HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap and also some Javascript. My main target right now is to start a bigger project, based mostly on JS because this is the best and also the fastest way to gain certain skills. That's why I'm looking for my first official job in the IT industry.
